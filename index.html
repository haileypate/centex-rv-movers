About

Ken Simmons and Dan Bristow have over 40 years combined experience in the transportation and highway safety industries. Both Ken and Dan have received multiple safe driver awards. They both learned to drive large vehicles while serving in the military and still love to hit the open road and meet friends old and new.

Ken is an experienced RV traveler and Dan enjoys renting different RVs for his travels.

CenTex RV Movers was started with the goal of helping others in meeting their RV transportation needs.
